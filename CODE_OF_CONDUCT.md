# VoidSlate Code of Conduct

## Our standard

The VoidSlate community should be respectful, constructive, and welcoming.

Examples of encouraged behavior:

- Giving helpful feedback.
- Respecting different skill levels and backgrounds.
- Reporting issues clearly and calmly.
- Keeping discussions focused on improving VoidSlate.

Examples of unacceptable behavior:

- Harassment, threats, insults, or discrimination.
- Personal attacks or hostile comments.
- Spam, trolling, or repeated off-topic arguments.
- Sharing private information without permission.
- Misrepresenting commercial permission or ownership of VoidSlate.

## Enforcement

The maintainer may remove comments, close issues, reject contributions, block users, or take other reasonable steps to protect the project community.
