# VoidSlate FAQ

## Is VoidSlate free?

Yes. VoidSlate is free for non-commercial use under the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0.

## Can I modify VoidSlate?

Yes, for permitted non-commercial purposes under the license.

## Can I share my modified version?

Yes, for permitted non-commercial purposes, as long as you include the license terms and required notices.

## Can I sell VoidSlate or a modified version?

No. Commercial use, resale, paid redistribution, or inclusion in commercial products or services requires prior written permission from MrakBook - Boris Karaoglanov.

## Can I use VoidSlate at work?

Commercial or company use may require written permission. Review [`COMMERCIAL_USE.md`](COMMERCIAL_USE.md) and the PolyForm Noncommercial License 1.0.0 in [`LICENSE.md`](LICENSE.md).

## Is VoidSlate open source?

VoidSlate is source-available and free for non-commercial use. Because commercial use is restricted, it should not be described as OSI-approved open source.

## Does VoidSlate collect data?

VoidSlate is intended to be a theme-only extension and does not intentionally collect personal data, telemetry, analytics, or source code. See [`PRIVACY.md`](PRIVACY.md).
